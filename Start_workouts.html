Jumping Jacks

60 Sec

Jumping jacks are a full body workout that can target major muscle groups, strengthen bones, and improve cardiovascular fitness and heart health

Squats

60 Sec

Squats burn calories and might help you lose weight. They also lower your chances of injuring your knees and ankles.

Back Lunges

60 Sec

Lunges strengthen your back and core muscles without putting too much stress or strain on your spine. A stable core reduces your chance of injury and improves your posture, making movements easier.

Side Lunges

60 Sec

Lateral lunges develop balance, stability, and strength. They work your inner and outer thighs and may even help to reduce the appearance of cellulite.

Forward Lunges

60 Sec

The forward lunge works muscle groups in your legs like the hamstrings, quadriceps, hip flexor muscles, gluteus maximus, and adductor muscles in your inner thighs.

Rest

60 Sec

Adequate rest between sets helps to maintain a high level of force production for the next set.
